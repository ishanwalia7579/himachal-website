District


  • Bilaspur:

    • Known for its picturesque landscapes and the Bhakra Nangal Dam, a major hydroelectric project.

  • Chamba:

    • Famous for its ancient temples, vibrant local culture, and scenic beauty.

  • Hamirpur:

    • A small district with notable educational institutions and vibrant local traditions.

  • Kangra:

    • Home to the beautiful Kangra Fort and renowned for its tea gardens and temples.

  • Kinnaur:

    • h1 Noted for its stunning mountain scenery and diverse cultural heritage.

  • Kullu:

    • Known for the Kullu Valley, which is a popular tourist destination with a range of adventure activities.

  • Lahaul and Spiti:

    • Characterized by its rugged terrain and Buddhist monasteries, it offers unique high-altitude landscapes.

  • Mandi:

    • Renowned for its historical temples and vibrant cultural festivals, as well as being an important commercial center.

  • Shimla:

    • The state capital, known for its colonial architecture and as a popular hill station.

  • Sirmaur:

    • Famous for its royal heritage and picturesque landscapes, including the town of Nahan.

  • Solan:

    • Known as the "Mushroom City of India" and famous for its educational institutions and pleasant climate.

  • Una:

    • Noted for its industrial development and being a key transportation hub.
